Messenger

Messenger was made by Facebook which is the world’s biggest messaging app. Although it may not have a lot of unique features, it is very user friendly and simple to use and it is safe to say a majority of people use it, which makes it easier. You can easily make a group chat or even challenge your friend’s to a mini messenger game.

Registering for the App

Step 1: You need to create a facebook account and you then use the same Facebook log in details to sign into messenger.

Step 2: If you have a Facebook account you have messenger with it.

Step 3: If you don't want to have a Facebook account you can deactivate your Facebook account and you will still have your messenger account.

Cautions

When it comes to social media in general, some basic things should be known when setting up an account and actually using them: Never share your password to anyone, even if your account is private, it could still reach a wider audience and think about what you post. One thing to note on Messenger, anyone can message you. It is best if you block those people if they ask for personal details or just harrass you in general. Also be careful of the info you post on facebook as it also reveals the same details on your Messenger.
