Instagram

Instagram is regarded as one of the most user friendly and simple apps to use and it is no wonder why it is one of the most popular social media apps to use in the world. Due to how easy and friendly the app is, it is easy to sign up with an account and post anything you like or share hobbies with the people you follow or even close friends. Because of this, it is a brilliant opportunity to use this app for education in a learning environment.

Registering for the App

Step 1: When you open the app, select sign up to be redirected to the account sign-up.

Step 2: Enter all of your details and hit the sign-up button.

Step 3: After registering, you can set up a profile picture, bio and add people you know.

Cautions

When it comes to social media in general, some basic things should be known when setting up an account and actually using them: Never share your password to anyone, even if your account is private, it could still reach a wider audience and think about what you post. One of the easiest ways to stay protected on Instagram is to make your account private so the only people that could view your posts is when you accept their follow request. However, people could still see your profile picture and bio regardless. If someone is harrassing you, it is pretty simple to blok them and report them to Instagram.

Educational Uses:

  • You can create an account specifically for school and add all your fellow classmates and teacher, in which you can post the progress of your work or even ask for assistance from your classmates and teacher with ease.
  • Instagram is also really good for its explore page which can recommend you pages that are similar to what you post and are interested, which can offer you some unexpected help.
  • In the cases of you being sick or just unable to arrive to class, you can always post homework through instagram!
  • In the case of a pandemic (such as now), the teacher can also post your homework and communicate with students through instagram which can prove very useful.
