Twitter

Twitter is a huge platform that has been used almost as long as Facebook and is one of the world’s top used social media platforms and still remains very popular to this day. Twitter works through hashtags and you can post stuff through tweets or you can privately message an individual. Due to the use of hashtags, it is easier to get through to a specific audience.

Registering for the App

Step 1: When you open the app, select sign up to be redirected to the account sign-up.

Step 2: Enter all of your details and hit the 'next' button.

Step 3: Enter a password for said account. Upon entering it, your account has been created.

How to enrol/use the app:

You can use the explore page to find poular topics online and is usually referred to as the 'trending tab'.

On your profile page, you can update your profile pic or background and customize your profile however you want it to be.

This is the settings page where you can change your email, block other accounts from following you and more.

You can also directly talk to people with the direct message or 'DM' function as well so you can have a full convocation with people you know or other people from around the world it’s a another great way to interact with people.

Cautions

When it comes to social media in general, some basic things should be known when setting up an account and actually using them: Never share your password to anyone, even if your account is private, it could still reach a wider audience and think about what you post. On twitter, you can protect your tweets which only allows the people you follow to view your tweets. Although, people can still access your account and see your profile picture and see your bio. You can also block users which doesnt allow them to see anything on your account except for your profile picture.

Educational Uses:

  • Teacher’s could make good use of this app as the use of hashtags would allow them to communicate directly with a group of students.
  • Through hashtags, students could send any work easily and it would be easy for the teacher to view everything sent.
  • If students or the teacher have any queries with the other people in the class, they can always privately message one another.
