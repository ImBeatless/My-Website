Cautions

When it comes to social media in general, some basic things should be known when setting up an account and actually using them: Never share your password to anyone, even if your account is private, it could still reach a wider audience and think about what you post. In 'channels' they can sometimes become more of an immature group chat than a professional outlet where groups can share files, images, videos etc. It can also become a place for bullying but there isnt a lot of it documented which is a positive.
